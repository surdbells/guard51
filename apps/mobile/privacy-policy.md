# Guard51 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

## Data We Collect
- Account information (name, email, phone) — provided by your employer
- Location data — collected during active shifts for patrol verification
- Device information — for push notifications and crash reporting
- Photos — only when you capture incident evidence

## How We Use Data
- Location: verify clock-in at assigned sites, track patrol compliance
- Photos: attach to incident reports
- Device tokens: deliver push notifications for shift changes and alerts

## Data Sharing
We share data only with your employing security company's administrators.
We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## Data Retention
Data is retained while your account is active. Contact your company admin
or email privacy@guard51.com for deletion requests.
